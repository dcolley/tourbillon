# Launch Campaign Plan

## Launch Objective

Execute a coordinated product launch to maximize awareness and initial adoption.

## Launch Timeline

| Phase | Date | Activities |
|-------|------|------------|
| Pre-Launch | T-2 weeks | Teaser content, influencer outreach, press list finalization |
| Launch Week | T-0 | Product announcement, social blitz, email blast, press release |
| Post-Launch | T+1 week | Follow-up content, customer testimonials, performance review |

## Launch Channels

### Press & Media
- Press release distribution
- Media outreach to relevant publications
- Pitch exclusive stories to key journalists

### Social Media
- Coordinated announcement across all platforms
- Teaser campaign (T-7 days)
- Launch day live posting schedule
- User-generated content campaign

### Email Marketing
- Announcement email to existing list
- Segmented outreach to warm leads
- Follow-up nurture sequence

### Paid Promotion
- Launch week ad boost on key platforms
- Retargeting campaign for site visitors
- Lookalike audience expansion

## Key Messages

1. **Primary:** [Tool] solves [key problem] for [target audience]
2. **Secondary:** [Key differentiator] sets us apart
3. **Supporting:** [Social proof or early results]

## Success Metrics

- Press mentions: Target [X] outlets
- Social reach: Target [X] impressions
- Email open rate: Target [X]%
- Website traffic: [X]% increase during launch week
- Sign-ups/registrations: Target [X]

## Post-Launch Review

- Compile performance data across all channels
- Document lessons learned
- Identify follow-up opportunities
- Plan next phase of marketing activities
