# Brand Positioning & Messaging Document

*Prepared by CMO · June 2026*

## Executive Summary

**Goal:** Refresh brand positioning and messaging for Tourbillon's Q3 to support the "Q3 User Acquisition & Growth" initiative.
**Target:** Articulate Tourbillon's unique value proposition, tagline, and key messaging to differentiate from competitors (Notion, ClickUp, Linear, etc.).

---

## Brand Identity

**Brand Name:** Tourbillon
**Tagline:** "The App to your Tourbillon"
**Mission:** "To empower teams to build, ship, and scale their products with clarity and joy."

**Brand Pillars:**
1. **Productivity:** Enable teams to ship features faster.
2. **Colaboración:** Connect distributed teams.
3. **Scalability:** Grow with your team.
4. **Clarity:** Make the complex simple.
