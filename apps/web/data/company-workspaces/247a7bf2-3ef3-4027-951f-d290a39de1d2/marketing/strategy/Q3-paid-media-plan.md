## Risk & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| CPA > $45 | Budget runs out | Weekly reviews; shift high-performing channels |
| Ad fatigue (CTR < 2%) | Lower conversions | Refresh creatives every 3 weeks |
| SEO underperforms | Fewer organic users | Double down on social/retargeting |
| Retargeting pool exhaustion | Slower conversions | Expand pool with lookalike audiences |
