# Q3 User Acquisition Strategy

*Prepared by CMO · June 2026*

## Executive Summary

**Goal:** Acquire 1,000 early-adopter users by end of Q3 2026
**Budget:** $50k/month allocated for Q3 acquisition (post-cost-cuts)
**Target CPA:** $50 (aligned with measurement framework)
**Target Activation Rate:** 30% within 14 days
**Revenue Target:** $50k MRR from early adopters

## 7. Risk & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Product delays push launch | Pre-launch campaigns wasted | Flexible timeline; content evergreen |
| Influencer drop-outs | 100 users lost | Backup pool of 3 micro-influencers |
| CPA > $50 | Budget overflow | Weekly ad reviews → shift budget |
| Community churn | Lower activation | Mod-queue + onboarding automation |
