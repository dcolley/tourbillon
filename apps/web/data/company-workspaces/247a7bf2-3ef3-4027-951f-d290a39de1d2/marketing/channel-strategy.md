# Channel Strategy

## Channel Overview

| Channel | Priority | Role | Status |
|---------|----------|------|--------|
| SEO / Organic | High | Long-term traffic growth | Active |
| Paid Media | High | Accelerated acquisition | Active |
| Content Marketing | High | Authority building | Active |
| Analytics | Medium | Measurement & optimization | Active |

## Channel Details

### SEO / Organic Search

- **Objective:** Drive sustainable organic traffic
- **Tactics:** Keyword research, on-page optimization, content creation
- **KPIs:** Organic traffic, keyword rankings, domain authority

### Paid Media

- **Objective:** Accelerate user acquisition through paid channels
- **Tactics:** Search ads, social ads, display retargeting
- **KPIs:** CAC, ROAS, click-through rate, conversion rate

### Content Marketing

- **Objective:** Build authority and engage target audience
- **Tactics:** Blog posts, guides, case studies, social content
- **KPIs:** Engagement, shares, backlinks, time on page

### Analytics & Tracking

- **Objective:** Measure performance and inform decisions
- **Tactics:** Dashboard setup, event tracking, reporting
- **KPIs:** Data accuracy, reporting timeliness, insight quality

## Channel Integration

All channels should work together to create a cohesive customer journey:

1. **Awareness:** Paid media + social drives initial exposure
2. **Consideration:** SEO content + email nurtures interest
3. **Conversion:** Landing pages + retargeting drives action
4. **Retention:** Content + analytics informs ongoing engagement

## Resource Allocation

- 40% Content & SEO
- 35% Paid Media
- 15% Analytics & Tools
- 10% Testing & Innovation
