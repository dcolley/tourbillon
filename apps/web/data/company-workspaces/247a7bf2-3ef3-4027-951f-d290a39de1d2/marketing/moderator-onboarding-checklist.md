# Community Moderator onboarding Checklist

This checklist guides new community members through their onboarding period.

---

## Pre-Onboarding (Before Day 1)

- [x] Application reviewed by CEO and Community Council
- [ ] Welcome email sent with Discord invite, onboarding session details, and 30-day probation information

---

## Day 1: Documentation & Self-Study

- [ ] Read full [Community Guidelines](/marketing/community-guidelines.md) (v1.0 Final)
- [ ] Explore all 4-4

## Week 1: Guidened Practice

- [ ] Pair with an existing moderator and shadow for 24 hours
- [ ] Submit 2-3 observations in a post-onboarding survey (Google Form) with ratings of justice and team.
