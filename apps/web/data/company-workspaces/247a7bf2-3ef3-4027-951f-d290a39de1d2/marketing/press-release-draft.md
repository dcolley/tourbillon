### Body Structure

#### 1. Dateline & Lede (Hook)
> `[CITY, Date]` — Tourbillon today announced its official launch of an AI agent orchestration platform designed to democratize complex task automation for teams and businesses worldwide. Built on a foundation of **Productivity** and **Simplicity**, Tourbillon enables any user — technical or non-technical — to delegate, automate, and execute multi-step workflows through natural conversation with autonomous agents.

#### 2. The Problem (Why Tourbillon)
- Complex automation tools require developers, analysts, and domain experts.
- Teams waste hours on repetitive cross-system tasks that could be automated in minutes.
- Existing solutions are either too manual or too complex to deploy at scale.

#### 3. The Solution — Tourbillon's Value Propositions
##### Key Value Prop #1: Productivity
> "Tourbillon multiplies team output by turning one person's intent into many agents' coordinated action."
- Agents plan, execute, and deliver on multi-step tasks autonomously.
- Parallel agent execution cuts task completion time from hours to minutes.
- Seamless handoff between specialized agents — each bringing domain expertise.

##### Key Value Prop #2: Simplicity
> "If you can explain it in a sentence, Tourbillon can execute it."
- Natural-language task submission — no code required.
- Visual project and goal tracking provides transparency without complexity.
- Non-technical users can lead automation initiatives from day one.

#### 4. How It Works (3 Steps)
1. **Describe** your goal in plain language via the web interface.
2. **Tourbillon plans and delegates** — agents break it down, assign subtasks, and execute.
3. **Review results** — everything is tracked visually; you can intervene at any step.

#### 5. Quotes
> *"We built Tourbillon because we believed powerful automation shouldn't require a PhD in engineering."*  
> — Founder & CEO, Tourbillon

> *"Our agents don't just follow instructions — they reason, adapt, and deliver results that matter."*  
> — CTO, Tourbillon
