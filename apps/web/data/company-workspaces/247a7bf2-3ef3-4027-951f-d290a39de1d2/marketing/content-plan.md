# Content Plan

## Content Pillars

1. **Product Education** - How-to guides, tutorials, feature spotlights
2. **Industry Insights** - Market trends, thought leadership
3. **Customer Stories** - Case studies, testimonials, use cases
4. **Company News** - Updates, milestones, announcements

## Content Calendar

| Week | Type | Topic | Channel | Owner |
|------|------|-------|---------|-------|
| 1 | Blog Post | Introduction to [Tool] | Blog + Social | CMO |
| 2 | Guide | Getting Started Guide | Blog + Email | CMO |
| 3 | Case Study | Early Adopter Story | Blog + Social | CMO |
| 4 | Video | Product Demo | YouTube + Social | CMO |
| 5 | SEO Article | [Target Keyword] | Blog | CMO |
| 6 | Webinar | Live Q&A Session | Email + Social | CMO |

## Content Distribution

- **Blog:** Primary hub for all long-form content
- **Social Media:** Amplification and community engagement
- **Email:** Nurturing and direct communication
- **YouTube:** Video content and tutorials

## SEO Strategy

- Target primary and secondary keywords per piece
- Optimize title tags, meta descriptions, and headers
- Build internal links between related content
- Pursue external backlinks through outreach

## Content Quality Standards

- Minimum 1,000 words for blog posts
- Clear headings and subheadings
- Visual elements (images, screenshots, graphics)
- Actionable takeaways
- Updated within 6 months of publication

## Measurement

- Track content performance by type and topic
- Monitor organic traffic growth
- Measure engagement metrics (time on page, bounce rate)
- Report monthly on content ROI
