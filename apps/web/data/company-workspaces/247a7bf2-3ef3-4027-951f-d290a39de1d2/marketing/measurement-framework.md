Parameters: `method_type` (e.g., `google`, `manual`), `page_location`.\n- `demo_requested`: Triggered when the demo request form is submitted.\n  - Parameters: `product_type` (e.g., `pro`, `enterprise`), `user_source`.\n- `document_download`: Triggered when a resource is downloaded.\n  - Parameters: `file_name`, `file_extension`, `file_type`.\n\n#### **Conversion goals**: Define primary and secondary conversion events in GA4 and CRM.\n#### **Cross-domain tracking**: Configure if checkout and site are on different domains.\n\n## Reporting Cadence\n| Report | Frequency | Audience | Owner |\n|--------|-----------|----------|--------|\n| Weekly Performance | Weekly | CMO | CMO |\n| Monthly Dashboard | Monthly | CEO | CMO |\n| Quarterly Review | Quarterly | CEO + Board | CMO |\n\n## Optimization Process\n1. **Monitor:** Track KPIs against targets weekly.\n2. **Analyze:** Identify trends, outliers, and opportunities.\n3. **Hypothesize:** Formulate testable hypotheses for improvement.\n4. **Test:** Run A/B tests and experiments.\n5. **Implement:** Roll out winning variations.\n6. **Document:** Record learnings in this framework.\n\n## A/B Testing Priorities\n| Test | Priority | Hypothesis | Status |\n|------|----------|------------|--------|\n| Landing Page CTA | High | Clearer CTA increases conversion | Planned |\n| Email Subject Lines | Medium | Personalization improves open rate | Planned |\n| Ad Creative Variations | High | Video ads outperform static | Planned |\n\n## Data Governance\n- All data collected in compliance with privacy regulations (GDPR/CCPA).\n- Regular data quality audits.\n- Clear data retention policies.\n- Access controls for sensitive information.
