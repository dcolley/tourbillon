## Council Duties

Each council member commits to:
- Attending at least 1 monthly Council sync (virtual)
- Reviewing escalated reports within 2 business days
- Contributing to quarterly transparency reports
- Participating in the rotation review at the end of the term

### Rotation Policy

- **Term length**: 3 months (quarterly)
- **Renewal**: Members may serve up to 2 consecutive terms (6 months)
- **Rotation review**: 2 weeks before term end, Council Chair assesses availability and performance
- **Succession**: Outgoing members nominate and onboarding candidate before departure
- **Vacancies**: Council Chair fills interim vacancies; full Council Vote on successor within 30 days
