## Product Hunt Description

**The Problem**
Modern workflows are drowning in ad-hoc AI agents, scattered APIs, and fragile integrations. Teams waste cycles herding cats — launching, scaling, and debugging AI agents that should be working together.

**The Solution**
Tourbillon is an open-source AI agent orchestration platform that unifies your entire agent ecosystem. Define, launch, monitor, and scale AI agents with a single source of truth — a vortex of agents that never stops.

**Why it matters:**
- **Zero-config onboarding:** Spin up your first agent in 90 seconds
- **Built-in observability:** Real-time dashboards for every agent's heartbeat
- **Modular architecture:** Hot-swap agents, tools, and data pipelines without downtime
- **Enterprise-grade security:** Role-based access, audit logs, and encrypted secrets
