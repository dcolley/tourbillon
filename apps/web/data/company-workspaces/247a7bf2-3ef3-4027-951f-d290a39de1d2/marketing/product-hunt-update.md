## Story (Main Description)

### The Problem

Most teams face one of three frustrations:

1. **AI tools are either too simple or too complex.** Zapier and Make handle basic task chains but can't reason. LangChain and CrewAI offer deep power — but require engineers and months to ship.
2. **You're stuck with single-agent solutions.** Copilot helps one person. What about workflows that need multiple AI specialists collaborating on a single problem?
3. **Prototypes never reach production.** Teams build agent prototypes in notebooks and scripts — then deployment infrastructure, scaling, and monitoring become the bottleneck.

**Tourbillon solves all three.** We combine visual multi-agent orchestration with human-in-the-loop control, team collaboration, and one-click deployment — from prototype to production without writing a line of code.

---

### What Is Tourbillon?

Tourbillon is an **open-source multi-agent orchestration platform** that brings AI automation from developer notebooks into the hands of PMs, ops teams, and subject matter experts.

**What you can do with Tourbillon:**

- **Design workflows visually** — Drag-and-drop agents onto a canvas, connect them with simple lines, define what each agent does, when they act, and how they communicate. No code required.

- **Orchestrate multi-agent handoffs** — Agents don't just execute rules. They reason about each step, pass rich context — including confidence scores and recommended actions — to the next agent in the chain.

- **Keep humans in the loop** — Place approval gates anywhere in your workflow. Your team gets a notification, reviews the agent's work, approves with one click, or sends back revision feedback. Full autonomy with full control.

- **Deploy with one click** — The platform handles auto-scaling, load balancing, and error monitoring. You get a unified dashboard showing every agent's activity, performance metrics, and real-time errors.

- **Collaborate as a team** — Shared workspaces with role-based access control (Admin, Editor, Viewer), available for your team to build and manage workflows together.

- **Stay compliant** — SOC 2 ready, GDPR/CCPA compliant, RBAC, and comprehensive audit trails out of the box.
