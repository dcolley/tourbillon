## ❓ Pricing FAQ Section

**Q: Can I switch plans later?**  
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your workspace settings. Prorated billing applies to upgrades; downgrades take effect at the end of the current cycle.

**Q: What happens when my free trial ends?**  
Your workspace goes read-only after 14 days on Pro trials (30-day enterprise demos). All data remains safe, and workflows pause until you subscribe. No automatic charges unless payment info is provided upfront.

**Q: Do I pay per agent or per workflow?**  
Tourbillon bills by *seats* (Pro/Enterprise) with generous active agent/workflow limits included. Heavy compute users can add modular compute packs without changing seat plans.

**Q: Is there a discount for startups or nonprofits?**  
Yes. We offer 50% off Pro plans for verified early-stage startups and registered nonprofits. Apply through our sales portal.
