## Overview

This document provides a standardized template and process for collecting, curating, and showcasing user testimonials across Tourbillon's marketing channels. Social proof is a critical trust signal — well-collected testimonials drive:
- **Website conversion** on landing pages and pricing pages
- **Sales conversations** with enterprise prospects
- **Community engagement** via featured success stories
- **Content marketing** (blog posts, case studies, social media)

### What Makes a Strong Testimonial?

| Element | Why It Matters |
|---------|----------------|
| **Specificity** — Named metrics, timeframes, outcomes | "Saved 8 hours/week" > "Made us more efficient" |
| **Authenticity** — Real name, role, company (or verifiable pseudonym) | Builds trust; avoids generic feel |
| **Context** — What problem they faced before Tourbillon | Creates a narrative arc: before → after |
| **Emotion/Impact** — How the result made them or their team feel | Connects logically with emotionally |

## How to Collect Testimonials

### Channel 1: In-App Survey (Post-Onboarding)

Trigger an automated survey at key milestones:
- **After first agent task completion** (Day 1–3)
- **After 7 days of active use**
- **After creating a second project or template**

Use the onboarding checklist ([`marketing/onboarding-checklist.md`](./onboarding-checklist.md)) engagement signals to time these prompts.

### Channel 2: Support Interactions (Post-Success)

When support tickets are resolved with high CSAT scores, agent teams can optionally request a testimonial as part of the closing communication.

### Channel 3: Community Spotlights

Feature active community helpers or power users in monthly spotlights — invite them to share what they've built on Tourbillon and how it impacted their workflow. These naturally become testimonials over time.

### Channel 4: Sales-Handoff Requests

When a sales conversation moves an enterprise prospect past the decision stage, ask for referrals who are already using Tourbillon (if the prospect has peers in other organizations).

## Testimonial Request Template

Use this template when requesting testimonials via email, in-app message, or direct outreach:

```markdown
Subject: We'd love to feature your story! ✨

Hi [First Name],

We noticed you've been doing some amazing work on Tourbillon — 
[optional: reference a specific achievement, e.g., "you completed 5 agent tasks this week" or "your template for weekly reporting got 12 saves"].

We're putting together testimonials from users like you to help other teams discover how powerful AI agent orchestration can be. Would you be open to sharing a few sentences about your experience?

Here are some prompts (answer as many or as few as you'd like):
- What was the biggest challenge you were facing before Tourbillon?
- How have you used Tourbillon, and what has been the impact on your workflow?
- If you had to describe Tourbillon in one sentence, what would it be?

No pressure at all — if you're not comfortable sharing publicly, we'd still love a quick quote for internal use!

Thanks for being part of the Tourbillon community. 🙏

Best,
[Name]
Tourbillon Team
```

## Showcase Format Examples

### Format 1: Quote Card (Website / Landing Page)

Used on homepage, pricing page, or feature pages as a trust signal.

> *"We used to spend 6 hours every Monday building status reports. Now Tourbillon agents handle it while I grab coffee — and the quality is actually better."*
> 
> **Jane Doe** · Head of Operations at [Company]  
> _Tourbillon user since March 2026_

### Format 2: Mini Case Study (Blog Post / Sales Deck)

1–2 page format with structured sections.

```markdown
## [User Name] — [Role], [Company]

**Challenge:** [Brief description of the problem they faced before Tourbillon, 2–3 sentences]

**Tourbillon Solution:** [How they used Tourbillon to address it, specific features/workflows mentioned]

**Results:** 
- 📊 Metric 1 (e.g., "Reduced report generation time from 6 hours to 15 minutes")
- 📊 Metric 2 (e.g., "Scaled from 1 agent to 12 agents across the team")
- 📊 Metric 3 (e.g., "Saved an estimated $Xk per quarter in manual labor costs")

**Quote:** "[Full testimonial quote — 2–4 sentences]"

— [Name], [Title] at [Company]
```

### Format 3: Social Media Snippet (Twitter / LinkedIn)

Short, punchy format optimized for sharing.

> 🚀 "Tourbillon didn't just automate our workflow — it changed how we think about work itself."  
> 
> Read what @[Handle/Name] had to say → [Link]
> 
> \#AI #Automation #Productivity #Tourbillon

### Format 4: Video Testimonial Brief

For users who prefer recording a short video (60–90 seconds). Provide this question guide:

```markdown
1. Tell us your name, role, and what your company does.
2. What problem were you trying to solve before Tourbillon?
3. How did you discover Tourbillon, and what drew you in?
4. Walk us through how you use Tourbillon now — what are you most proud of?
5. What has been the biggest impact (metric or qualitative) on your team/company?
6. If someone is on the fence about trying Tourbillon, what would you tell them?
```

## Usage Guidelines & Best Practices

### Approval Process

All testimonials must be reviewed before publication:

| Step | Action | Owner |
|------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Collect testimonial via request template or organic channel | Support / Marketing / Community |
| 2 | Draft showcase in the format above | Marketing |
| 3 | Share draft with the user for review and approval | Test CEO / CMO |
| 4 | Approve, revise, or decline; update tracking sheet | User + Marketing |
| 5 | Publish to designated channels | Marketing (with Test CEO sign-off) |

### Tracking Sheet Template

Maintain a master spreadsheet at `marketing/testimonial-tracking.csv` with the following columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `User Name` | Full name (or pseudonym if anonymous requested) |
| `Role / Company` | Job title and organization |
| `Status` | Requested → Received → Approved → Published |
| `Format` | Quote Card / Case Study / Social / Video |
| `Channel(s)` | Where it's published (homepage, blog, LinkedIn, etc.) |
| `Date Collected` | When testimonial was received |
| `Date Published` | When it went live |
| `Impact Metric` | Specific numbers mentioned in the testimonial |
| `Approver` | Who approved for publication |
| `Expiry Review` | Date to re-check if testimonial still accurate (6–12 months out) |

### Legal & Compliance Notes

- **Consent:** Always obtain explicit written consent before publishing a user's name, photo, or company affiliation. If the user requests anonymity, use only their first initial and role (e.g., "J.D., Product Manager").
- **Accuracy:** Never fabricate, exaggerate, or materially alter quotes. Use ellipses (...) if trimming longer statements for space — only remove filler, not meaning.
- **Metrics:** Verify any quantitative claims referenced in the testimonial are attributable to the user's experience (avoid implying universal results).
- **IP:** User-contributed content shared via testimonials grants Tourbillon a non-exclusive license to display it on marketing channels, per our Terms of Service (§5.2 — Feedback).

### Rotation & Refresh Schedule

- Homepage quote cards: rotate quarterly to keep social proof fresh
- Case studies: publish 1–2 per quarter (align with major product updates or milestones)
- Social media snippets: feature rotating testimonials alongside content from the [content plan](./content-plan.md)
- Video testimonials: target 1 per quarter for YouTube and LinkedIn
