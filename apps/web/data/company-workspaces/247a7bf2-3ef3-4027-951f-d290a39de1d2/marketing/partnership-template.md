# Partnership — PARTNERSHIP TEMPLATE

## Overview
This is a template for outreach email for potential partners to drive collaborative growth efforts. Customize [bracketed] fields per partner.

---

## Email Template

**Subject Line:** Partner with [Partner Company Name] — Let's Automate Together

**Body:**

Hi [Partner's First Name],

I'm reaching out because we see a strong opportunity for [Tourbillon] and [Partner Company Name] to collaborate and deliver even more value to our shared customers.

### About Tourbillon
Tourbillon is an AI-agent orchestration platform that helps teams automate workflows, manage goals, and scale productivity. We currently serve [X]+ users across [SaaS / Agency / Enterprise] verticals.

### Why Partner With Us?
- **Shared Audience:** Both our customers are [description of overlapping personas]
- **Complementary Products:** Tourbillon agents can seamlessly integrate with [Partner Product], and vice versa
- **Mutual Value:** Cross-promotion, co-marketing, and even API-based automations

### Collaboration Ideas
1. **Product Integrations** — Native [Partner] agent or webhooks (e.g., "Auto-create project from [Partner] task")
2. **Co-Marketing** — Webinar, joint blog post, or case study showcasing combined value
3. **Referral Program** — Cross-promote to each other's customer bases
4. **Bundled Offering** — Joint pricing page or "Starter Pack"

### Proposed Next Steps
- **Discovery Call:** 30-minute call to align on mutual priorities
- **Integration Scope:** Quick technical feasibility assessment
- **Co-Marketing Calendar:** Draft content, webinars, and launches

📅 **Let's schedule a quick call?** [Link to Calendly or availability]

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title] | Tourbillon
[Email] | [Phone] | [LinkedIn]
