# Q1 Budget Allocation & Cost-Cutting Plan

## Current Budget Status
- **Monthly Budget:** $500k/month
- **Tokens Spent:** ~86.3M (current month)
- **Budget Ratio:** ~172% of monthly average
- **Budget Exhausted:** No (but high utilization)

## Q1 Spend Review

### Cost Drivers Identified
1. **Paid Media:** ~40% of spend (Facebook, LinkedIn, Google Ads)
2. **Content Production:** ~25% (Blogs, Videos, Design assets)
3. **Tooling & SaaS:** ~15% (Analytics, CRM, Email, Social scheduling)
4. **Influencer/PR:** ~12% (Product Hunt, Beta readers, Reviewers)
5. **Community Dev:** ~5% (Discord, Slack, Community managers)
6. **Contingency/Misc:** ~3% (Design, Copy, Enterprise pilots)

### Cost-Saving Initiatives

| Category | Initiative | Expected Savings | Implementation Timeline |
|----------|------------|-------------------|--------------------------|
| Paid Media | Pause low-ROI platforms (experiment with $100/day CPA targets) | $60k/mo | Immediate |
| Content | Shift to user-generated/barbell | -$25k/mo | Week 1 |
| SaaS | Downgrade "Staging" environments (QA, Dev) to "Production" | $15k/mo | Week 2 |
| PR | Swap influencer endorsements for Affiliate/Partner bundles | $12k/mo | Week 2 |
| Community | Vol. leverage for on-boarding/triaging | $5k/mo | Week 3 |

**Total Estimated Monthly Savings:** ~$7M/month
**Q1 Remaining Allocation:** Re-allocate savings to high-ROI experiments (e.g., Product Hunt, SEO, Beta conversion incentives)
