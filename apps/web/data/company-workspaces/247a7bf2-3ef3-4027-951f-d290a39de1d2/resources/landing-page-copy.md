## How It Works (3-Step Process)

### Step 1: Design Your Workflow
Drag and drop agents onto your canvas. Define their capabilities, tools they can access, and how they communicate with each other. Choose from pre-built templates or start from scratch.

### Step 2: Configure & Test
Fine-tune agent behavior, set guardrails, connect to your data sources and APIs. Use Tourbillon's built-in simulation environment to test workflows before deployment.

### Step 3: Deploy & Monitor
Launch with one click. Tourbillon handles the infrastructure — auto-scaling, load balancing, error monitoring. Watch everything in real-time from a unified dashboard.
