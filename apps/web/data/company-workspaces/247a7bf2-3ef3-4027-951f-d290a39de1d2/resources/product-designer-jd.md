# Product Designer (UI/UX) - Job Description

## Role Summary
We are looking for a creative and detail-oriented **Product Designer** to own Tourbillon's UI/UX and lead our design system. You will craft intuitive, accessible interfaces that delight our community and turn complex AI workflows into simple, elegant user experiences.

## Responsibilities
- Own end-to-end UX/UI design for Tourbillon, from wireframe to high-fidelity mockup.
- Design and maintain our design system (tokens, components, variants) in Figma.
- Collaborate with the engineering team to translate Figma layouts into React components using TailwindCSS and Shadcn UI.
- Conduct user research, A/B tests, and usability sessions with our early adopters and Discord community.
- Iterate rapidly based on user feedback, analytics, and Product Hunt community input.

## Qualifications
- 3+ years of product design experience (SaaS, web apps, or dashboards preferred).
- Expert proficiency in Figma (auto layout, components, variables, and prototyping).
- Deep understanding of design systems and component-driven design.
- Strong grasp of responsive layouts, typography, and color theory.
- Familiarity with modern front-end stacks (React, TailwindCSS) and how designs are translated into code.
- Strong portfolio showcasing user flows, interactive prototypes, and well-documented component libraries.

## Nice-to-have
- Experience designing for AI, productivity, or developer tools.
- Basic animation or motion design skills (Lottie, Framer Motion).
- Experience building and launching a Product Hunt campaign.

## Salary & Benefits
- Salary range: $90,000 - $130,000 (Base) + Equity (based on experience).
- Remote-first (US / Global) with synchronous overlap.
- Generous PTO and complete flexibility.
- Joining a fast-growing startup in the AI-agent ecosystem.

---
## Application Process
1. **Apply:** Send your resume and Figma portfolio (spec or library preferred) to careers@tourbillon.ai
2. **Initial Screen:** 30-minute chat on design process, Figma, and role highlights.
3. **Portfolio Deep-Dive:** 60-minute walk-through of one campaign/project.
4. **Hands-on Take-home:** Create a wireframe for a specific Tourbillon user flow (approx. 2-3 hours).
5. **Final Round:** 45-minute design critique with the CEO and CTO (React/Tailwind translation focus).

## About Tourbillon
Tourbillon is the open-source platform designed for AI-powered workflows, automating complex processes, and launching AI-agent systems quickly. Built on a solid tech stack (Node.js, Express, React, PostgreSQL), we bring a minimal-dev team approach, designing and building smart systems.
